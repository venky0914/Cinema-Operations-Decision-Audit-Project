# 🎬 Cinema Operations Decision Audit  
End-to-End Data Analytics Project | EDA • Business Insights • Decision Audit • BI Dashboards

---

## 🔍 Project Summary
This project performs an **end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and **Decision Audit** on cinema operations data to evaluate how **ticket pricing, show timing, occupancy, queue time, customer behavior, and movie performance** impact **revenue efficiency and customer experience**.

Unlike basic EDA projects, this analysis focuses on **validating business assumptions and recommending operational decisions** using data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement
Cinema operators often assume:
- Higher ticket prices always increase revenue  
- Higher occupancy always improves profitability  
- Peak showtimes automatically generate maximum profit  

This project **tests these assumptions using real operational data** and identifies **what to optimize, what to stop, and what to scale**.

---

## 🧠 Business Objectives
- Optimize ticket pricing strategy
- Improve revenue per seat
- Balance occupancy with queue time (customer experience)
- Identify high-performing show times and movie genres
- Understand customer snack spending behavior
- Support operational and pricing decisions using data

---

## 📊 Key KPIs & Metrics Analyzed
- Total Revenue  
- Average Ticket Price  
- Occupancy Rate  
- Revenue per Seat  
- Queue (Waiting) Time  
- Snack Spend per Customer  
- Show Time Performance  
- Ticket Price Range Efficiency  
- Customer Type Contribution  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & EDA Process
- Removed duplicate and inconsistent records  
- Handled missing values using **mean or median based on data distribution**
- Standardized categorical variables (Show Time, Customer Type, Movie Type)
- Created **derived business metrics**:
  - Revenue per Seat  
  - Occupancy Levels (Low / Medium / High)  
  - Ticket Price Bands  
- Conducted univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analysis
- Validated business assumptions through comparative visual analysis

---

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard – Key Insights
- **Medium ticket price range (₹150–₹220)** achieves:
  - Highest occupancy
  - Best revenue efficiency  
- **High ticket prices reduce occupancy**, lowering total revenue  
- **Evening shows** generate highest occupancy but also longest queue times  
- **Night shows** generate highest snack spend despite lower occupancy  
- **Individual customers** spend more on snacks than family customers  
- **Blockbuster movies** generate higher revenue per seat than non-blockbusters  
- Action and Drama genres dominate overall demand

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus pricing strategy on **medium ticket price bands**
- Improve queue management during **high-occupancy evening shows**
- Promote snack combos during **night shows**
- Optimize show scheduling instead of increasing screen count
- Prioritize **revenue efficiency over raw occupancy**
